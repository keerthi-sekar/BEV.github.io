About Us


Started in Fall 2019, Bearcat Electric Vehicle (BEV) is a student lead organization that will be building the University of Cincinnati's First Electric Vehicle. Students will have the opportunity to hone on their engineering and leadership skills while building an innovative car over the next year. Any donations will help senior engineers graduate with a BEV Senior Design Project. BEV will be supported by our IC team (BCMS) to absorb all the previous knowledge/experience. BCMS has been competing in FSAE since 1981, being one of the first teams to compete in the competition series.
