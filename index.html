Electric is the Future



Bearcat Electric Vehicle, University of Cincinnati's first all-electric formula racecar team. Our mission is to design, build, and iterate an electric racecar to compete at the annual International FSAE Competition Circuit while emphasizing sustainable transportation innovation and providing students with real-world engineering experience. 


How to get involved

Want to be apart of the team? Email us at bearcatev@uc.edu to get more information. Our Meetings are Sunday's at 8pm.
